Safety Standards Compliance Report:

"Implementation of standards in accordance with the regulations set by the Oil and Gas Regulatory Authority (OGRA)."

We are pleased to report that our facility has diligently implemented and adhered to the safety standards prescribed by the Oil and Gas Regulatory Authority (OGRA). Our commitment to ensuring a secure and compliant working environment is reflected in the following key areas:

Fire Water Spray System of Plant:
Our facility is equipped with a robust fire water spray system designed to swiftly respond to potential fire incidents, aligning with OGRA safety standards.

Bowzer Decanting Point:
The decanting point for our bowsers strictly follows OGRA regulations, ensuring safe and efficient transfer operations.

Storage Tanks:
All storage tanks on our premises adhere to OGRA standards for construction, maintenance, and safety protocols.

Cylinder Filling Shed:
Our cylinder filling shed meets OGRA specifications, guaranteeing secure handling and filling procedures for LPG cylinders.

Fire Fitting Drills:
Regular fire fitting drills are conducted to enhance our team's preparedness and response capabilities, aligning with OGRA guidelines.

Water Storage Capacity:
Our facility maintains a water storage capacity of 60,000 gallons, meeting OGRA requirements for emergency response and firefighting.

Flow Rate of 500 GPM for at Least 200 Hours (NFPA-58):
We adhere to the NFPA-58 standards, ensuring a flow rate of 500 gallons per minute for a minimum of 200 hours, reinforcing our commitment to safety and emergency preparedness.

For more detailed information on our safety practices, please feel free to contact us or visit our facility. Your safety is our top priority.

By implementing these measures, we affirm our dedication to meeting and exceeding the safety benchmarks set by OGRA. Our compliance is a testament to our unwavering commitment to the well-being of our employees, the community, and the environment.
